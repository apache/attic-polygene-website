Overview

The Polygene™ Libraries are of varying maturity level and we try to maintain a STATUS (dev-status.xml) file indicating how good the codebase, documentation and unit tests are for each of the libraries. This is highly subjective and potentially different individuals will judge this differently, but at least it gives a ballpark idea of the situation for our users.
